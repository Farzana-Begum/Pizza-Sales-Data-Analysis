###  Project Overview
This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover insights about customer ordering behavior, sales performance, and product trends.Using SQL for data extraction and Power BI for visualization, the analysis provides a clear view of key business metrics such as total revenue, order volume, and product performance.
###  Project Objectives
-	Analyze overall revenue and order trends.
-	Identify top-selling pizzas and sales categories.
-	Measure average order value and pizzas per order.
-	Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for actionable insights.

### Project Insights
-	Based on days orders are highest on weekends Friday/Saturday evening.
-	Based on Months there are maximum orders from month of January and July.
- Classic category pizza contributes to maximum sales and total orders
- Large size pizza contributes to maximum sales.
- The brie carre pizza contributes to minimum total orders and minimum total revenue.
### Final Conclusion
The analysis highlights key sales patterns and customer preferences that can help improve business decisions. Peak sales occur during weekends and specific months like January and July, indicating strong seasonal and weekend demand. The Classic category and Large-sized pizzas drive the highest revenue, making them core products to prioritize in marketing and inventory planning.
